THE COLORS OF THE RAINBOW

Red Red is the first color of the rainbow. When referring to the rainbow, it is associated to life. However, overall it symbolizes fire, warfare, and violence.

Orange Orange is the second color of the rainbow, in between red and yellow. When referring to the rainbow, it is associated to creativity and the ability to enjoy oneself. But mainly is associated with optimism and energy.

Yellow Yellow is the third color of the rainbow, in between orange and green. In the rainbow, it means the brilliance of thoughts and wisdom. It is mainly associated with sunshine, warmth, and happiness.

Green Green is the fourth color of the rainbow, in between yellow and blue. In the rainbow, it means healing. It is mainly associated with new beginnings and growth.

Blue Blue is the fifth color of the rainbow, in between green and indigo. In the rainbow, it means spirituality. It is mainly associated with freedom, intuition, and imagination. And may also mean depth, trust, and loyalty.

Indigo Indigo is the sixth color of the rainbow, in between blue and violet. In the rainbow, it represents the bridge between the subconscious and conscious worlds within the rainbow. It is mainly associated with wisdom and intuition.

Violet Violet is the seventh color of the rainbow. In the rainbow, it represents mercy. It can signify modesty and humility and can be seen as a sign of innocence.
